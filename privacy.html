Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Edgy Loops (“we”, “our”, or “us”) develops mobile applications available on Google Play and operates the website https://edgyloops.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our apps or website.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information unless you choose to provide it (for example, when sending feedback). However, our apps may automatically collect certain non-personal data through third-party SDKs. This may include:

  • Device information (model, operating system version, device language)
  • Interaction data (screens opened, features used, time spent)
  • Crash logs and diagnostics
  • Advertising identifiers (such as the Android Advertising ID)
  • IP address (used for security, fraud prevention, and analytics)

2. Third-Party Services We Use

To operate and improve our apps, we use trusted third-party services provided by Google:

  • Google Firebase Analytics – collects anonymous usage data to understand how users interact with our app.
  • Google Firebase Crashlytics – collects crash reports and performance data to help us diagnose issues.
  • Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK) – displays advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, approximate location, IP address, and interaction data to serve personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your Google settings.

These services may process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.
Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. How We Use Information

We use collected data to:

  • Improve app performance and stability
  • Diagnose crashes and technical issues
  • Analyze usage patterns to enhance features
  • Display advertisements and prevent fraud

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Data may be shared only with the third-party services listed above for analytics, crash reporting, or advertising purposes.

5. Data Retention

We retain anonymous analytics and crash data only as long as necessary to improve app performance and user experience.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our apps are not intended for children under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Your Choices

You may:

  • Disable personalized ads through your Google account settings
  • Request deletion of any data you voluntarily provided (such as feedback)
  • Contact us for questions or concerns regarding your privacy

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Any changes will be reflected here with an updated “Last updated” date.
