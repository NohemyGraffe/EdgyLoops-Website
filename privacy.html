Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

Edgy Loops (“we”, “our”, or “us”) operates mobile and web applications developed under the Edgy Loops brand. This page explains how we handle information when you use our apps or website.

1. Information we collect

Most of our apps do not require you to provide personal information. However, some apps may collect limited data such as:

  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • Anonymous usage analytics (for performance improvement)
  • Optional information you provide (for example, when sending feedback)

2. How we use information

We use collected data only to:

  • Improve app performance and user experience
  • Fix technical issues or bugs
  • Respond to user requests and feedback

3. Data sharing

We do not sell or share your personal information. In some cases, we may use trusted third-party services (such as Google Analytics or Firebase) strictly to analyze app performance.

4. Data retention

We keep information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy. Anonymous analytics data may be retained for aggregate statistics.

5. Children’s privacy

Our apps are not directed toward children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from them.

6. Your rights

You can request deletion of your data or ask questions about this policy by contacting us at admin@edgyloops.com.

7. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with a new “Last updated” date.

8. Contact

If you have any questions, reach out to us at admin@edgyloops.com.
